Dashing Krew

Release date : Arround 2025 / 2026

Dashign Krew is a game where a little alien has to find his way back to the mother ship using the abilities of the ufos of his friends, use them at your advantage to make your way through many dangers and, perhaps, go back home !

Trivia

You may wonder why I spelled Crew with a "K", the reason behind this is actually because this games take inspiration from the barrels section from Donkey Kong Country Returns, where Donkey Kong needs to hop between barrels to reach the end, like in this game, and you may notice that both the initials of Donkey Kong and Dashing Krew are, welp, "DK".
